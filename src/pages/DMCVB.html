From the very beginning, even before the automobile, Detroit has been a city of makers. We made carriages and stoves, we brewed beer, constructed ships, and eventually put the world on wheels. Detroiters aren’t afraid to get down and dirty, and that proud tradition is what carried us through to today. The return of Detroit has been fueled by makers, creatives, and people who aren’t afraid to roll up their sleeves and get down to work. These people have a passion for Detroit that runs deeper than just hometown spirit, and that passion is what has made Detroit great once again. And once you experience Detroit you’ll know right away; We made It.
